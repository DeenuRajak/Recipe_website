Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk powder
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons of yogurt
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • A few drops of rose water or cardamom powder for flavor
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, and a pinch of baking soda.
  2. Add 2 tbsp ghee and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Shape into small balls and fry in hot oil until golden.
  4. In a separate pan, boil 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water to make syrup.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the syrup for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Gulab Jamuns!

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Milk - 1 liter
  • Lemon juice or vinegar - 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Water - 4 cups
  • Cardamom pods - 4
  • Saffron strands - a pinch
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds - for garnishing

Recipe

  1. Boil the milk in a pan.
  2. Add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle the milk and strain it using a muslin cloth
  3. Knead the chenna until smooth and roll it into small balls.
  4. In another pan, make a syrup by combining sugar, water, and cardamom pods.
  5. Gently drop the chenna balls into the syrup, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Remove the Rasmalai from the syrup and let them cool.
  7. Garnish with saffron strands, chopped pistachios, and almonds.
  8. Chill in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving.

Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons ghee and a little milk at a time to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into smooth balls.
  4. In a deep pan, heat oil or ghee for frying and fry.
  5. In a separate pan, make a sugar syrup by dissolving 1 cup.
  6. Once the syrup reaches a sticky consistency, remove it from heat.
  7. Drop the fried gulab jamuns into the warm sugar.
  8. Garnish with chopped nuts like pistachios or almonds, and serve warm or chilled.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • Sliced almonds and pistachios for garnish

Recipe

  1. In a non-stick pan, heat ghee and add condensed milk. Cook on low heat for a few minutes.
  2. Add milk powder and cardamom powder to the pan.
  3. Mix well until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased plate and spread it evenly.
  5. Garnish with sliced almonds and pistachios, pressing them gently into the mixture.
  6. Let it cool and set for a few hours.
  7. Cut into desired shapes and serve the delicious Barfi!
